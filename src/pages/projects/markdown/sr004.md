# Introduction

The IN-SPACe Model Rocketry Competition 2026 (we shorten it to IMRC2026) is a competition organized by [IN-SPACe](https://www.inspace.gov.in/inspace) in collaboration with [ISRO](https://www.isro.gov.in) and [Shrinarayani Science Development Foundation](https://www.ssdf.org.in) to be help in Kushinagar, UP will consist the development of a Model Rocket to be designed to reach an apogee of 1000m($\pm$ 100m) AGL. The rocket will be deploying a CAN7U-SAT at apogee from another satellite team participating in the IN-SPACe Student CANSAT Competition 2026 also consist of an experimental module performing a scientific experiment.

Majority of the information will be open-sourced after the completion of the competition i.e., after September / November. To sponsor us and know what we are doing and to support the cause please consider supporting us [here](../../../pages/sponsor-tier) we really require funding and are very short on money.

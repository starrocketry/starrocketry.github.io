## MISSION OVERVIEW

The Nebula Core initiative represents a breakthrough in sustainable orbital energy. By utilizing cold-fusion reactors housed within reinforced glass-morphism shells, we can power entire satellite networks with zero carbon emission.

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

- **Reactor Type:** Fusion-Class C
- **Energy Output:** 4.2 Gigawatts
- **Cooling System:** Liquid Nitrogen Loop
- **Shielding:** Lead-lined Polycarbonate

## RECOVERY PHASES

1. Initial deployment of core casing.
2. Injection of tritium-deuterium fuel.
3. Ignition sequence via ground control telemetry.
